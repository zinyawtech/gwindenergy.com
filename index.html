About Us

At Good Wind Energy, we are shaping a brighter, cleaner future for Myanmar. With decades of experience in renewable energy, we specialize in unlocking the untapped potential of wind power in frontier markets.

Our team combines cutting-edge technology, deep local knowledge, and a passion for sustainability to deliver projects that meet international standards while addressing local needs.

From conducting comprehensive feasibility studies to fostering trust with communities, we take pride in our ability to navigate complex challenges and deliver results.

Our Projects

GWE is developing Myanmar’s first "shovel-ready" and internationally bankable wind power portfolio, demonstrating the vast potential of Myanmar’s wind energy sector.

Focused on a 500 MW portfolio across three strategic locations, these projects meet international standards for technical and environmental excellence, positioning Myanmar as a key player in the global wind power industry.

From conducting comprehensive feasibility studies to fostering trust with communities, we take pride in our ability to navigate complex challenges and deliver results.

Achievements in Myanmar Wind Power

Exclusive Partnerships: Secured MOUs with Myanmar’s Ministry of Electricity and Energy (MOEP) for feasibility studies across three project locations in two regions.

Data-Driven Development: Conducted over six years of wind measurement campaigns at five distinct locations.

Comprehensive Pre-Development: Completed feasibility studies, wind resource assessments, grid impact studies, transmission line routing, transportation studies, land surveys, and environmental/social impact assessments.

Capacity Building: Conducted workshops for ministries and local communities, enhancing awareness and acceptance of clean energy while supporting the development of wind power regulations aligned with international standards.
